Anesthesia Safety ECU Unit

CASE STUDY

  • HEALTHCARE

Business Overview

Germany-based Fortune 500 company with a large presence in Healthcare, specializing in medical device manufacturing and market leader in diagnostic imaging. They wanted to address all their challenges in order to get a device that significantly enhances patient safety, instills confidence in healthcare professionals, and improves overall healthcare outcomes in critical care settings. A Safety ECU was much in need which had to be capable of detecting and responding to potential issues promptly, implementing appropriate safety measures to mitigate risks to the patient’s well-being.

Challenge

The challenge for the customer lies in designing and developing an anaesthesia device that incorporates an internal embedded unit known as the Safety ECU. The primary objective of this device is to ensure the utmost safety of patients in the event of a failure in ventilation control parameters. 

Solution

The Safety Unit is considered an integral part of the anaesthesia machine and at the same time it is an independent module employed to monitor the safety of ventilation control parameters (pressure and flow), electrical parameters like power rails, sensor board voltage levels and pneumatic parameters like valve functional status, blower thermal level etc. It also monitors battery charging states and safety alarm generation to keep the device always in safe state.

Value Delivered

Our solution is able to monitor:

  • Safety of ventilation control parameters pressure and flow
  • Electrical parameters power rails and sensor board voltage levels
  • Pneumatic parameters valve functional status and blower thermal level
  • Battery charging states along with safety alarm generation
  • Monitoring and safety alarm generation keeps the device always in safe state.

ECG Device Revamp & Gateway Integration

CASE STUDY

  • HEALTHCARE

Business Overview

The customer is a Fortune 500 company headquartered in the United States with a significant presence in the healthcare sector. It specializes in the manufacturing and distribution of diagnostic medical equipment, focusing on cutting-edge health technology for medical imaging, information technologies, medical diagnostics, patient monitoring systems, and more.

Getting hold of a seamless platform to acquire, process, and analyze ECG data in real-time was an ongoing struggle! We leveraged our expertise and experience in this domain and developed a solution that can facilitate better patient monitoring and efficient collaboration among medical professionals, ultimately improving the quality of healthcare services provided.

Challenge

The challenge at hand involves developing a platform that can seamlessly acquire data from an ECG machine, perform efficient signal processing, and stream the processed data to a Graphical User Interface (GUI). Additionally, the platform must enable the smooth transfer of ECG files to a cloud server in the required format.

Solution

Our solution successfully implemented an Acquisition Module to acquire ECG data, ensuring accurate and reliable measurements. The acquired data was efficiently processed to enhance its quality and streamed in real-time to a Graphical User Interface (GUI). 

Additionally, our solution integrated a Thermal Printer for on-demand printing of ECG reports. To ensure seamless data storage and accessibility, we facilitated the transfer of ECG files to a cloud server in the necessary format. 

Value Delivered

  • ECG analysis & processing
  • ECG report generation with five types of formats
  • Continuous operation of 450 minutes with full charge battery without printing
  • USB interface for mass storage & HID
  • Ethernet & Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Provision for add on GSM module

Revolutionizing EMR with AI-Powered Voice Integration: NeST Digital’s Success Story with a Top Electronic Medical Record Software Provider in Canada

Revolutionizing EMR with AI-Powered Voice Integration: NeST Digital’s Success Story with a Top Electronic Medical Record Software Provider in Canada

CASE STUDY

  • HEALTHCARE

Business Overview

The client provides Electronic Medical Record (EMR) software solutions to healthcare practices in Canada. EMR systems are digital versions of patients’ healthcare charts that allow clinicians to create, store, and receive them. The client wanted to enhance their EMR software with advanced text-to-speech features, automation, and voice recognition that complement existing clinical workflows through their intuitive cloud-based platform. 

Challenge

The client’s objectives were to deliver a high-quality EMR software solution that meets the needs and expectations of their customers, but they faced several hurdles in achieving these goals: 

  • Integrating with third-party services such as blockchain networks, print/fax systems, and other EMR platforms 
  • Developing a cross-platform application that works seamlessly on Android, iOS, and web devices 
  • Reducing consultation time for doctors by enabling them to use voice commands instead of typing or clicking
  • Providing an intelligent prescription mechanism that checks for drug interactions, allergies, and dosage errors using natural language processing (NLP) and artificial intelligence (AI) 

 

Solution

NeST Digital leveraged its expertise in NLP, AI, React Native, and Microsoft Azure technologies to develop a virtual assistant for doctors that extends the voice interface of the client’s EMR software.

Features:

  • Voice interface extension for EMR using Microsoft Azure NLP features, such as Language Understanding Intelligent Service (LUIS) and Cognitive Services

 

  • An intelligent prescription mechanism that uses AI to generate prescriptions based on voice inputs from doctors and performs real-time medication-to-medication interaction checking using a blockchain-based prescription network 
  • Forward prescriptions to third-party integrations, including blockchain integration using Microsoft Azure cloud services 
  • Tenant management web interface that allows administrators to manage users, roles, and permissions for different branches or clinics 
  • Print/fax integration that enables doctors to print or fax prescriptions directly from their mobile devices or web browsers 

Value Delivered

NeST Digital delivered a robust and innovative EMR software solution that enhanced the client’s competitive edge in the Canadian healthcare market. The solution provided several benefits, such as: 

  • Improved efficiency and productivity of doctors by reducing consultation time and eliminating manual errors 
  • Increased patient safety and satisfaction by providing accurate and personalized prescriptions based on voice inputs from doctors 
  • Enhanced interoperability and scalability of the EMR software by integrating with various third-party services using Microsoft Azure cloud services 
  • Increased customer loyalty and retention by offering a user-friendly and cross-platform application that works on any device
