FliSAFE – Flight Data Monitoring and Analysis Software: NeST Digital’s Solution to Enhance Flight Safety and Aircraft Health Monitoring.

CASE STUDY

  • AEROSPACE AND DEFENCE

Business Overview

NeST Digital provided a Flight Data Monitoring (FDM) software solution to a customer who is a flight operator and is required to adhere to regulatory norms for Flight Operational Quality Assurance (FOQA). The customer needed a tool capable of analyzing and reporting data from Flight Data Recorder (Black box) to maintain flight safety standards, monitor aircraft health, and for accident/incident investigation purposes. The solution catered to the needs of Fixed Wing and Rotor Crafts for effective Flight/Helicopter Operational Quality Assurance.

Challenge

Flight operators face the challenge of ensuring flight safety and monitoring aircraft health while adhering to regulatory requirements, making implementing Flight Data Monitoring (FDM) and Flight Operational Quality Assurance (FOQA) norms complex. The customer aimed to manage risks associated with flight operations to improve business performance. They needed a highly customizable software tool to analyze and report data from any aircraft type and flight data recorder, meet DGCA approval standards, and match OEM software to ensure compliance and reliability.

Solution

NeST Digital conceptualized and developed a client-server architecture-based software solution that complied with DO-178 B Level E standard (Ground System Software). FliSAFE was the comprehensive Safety Management Flight Data Monitoring (FDM)/ Flight Data Analysis (FDA) tool designed to help airlines identify, classify, and manage risks for the safety of flight operations.

Critical features of FliSAFE include:

  • A customizable and secure software solution that supports any aircraft type and flight data recorder, employing client-server architecture and supporting multiple clients.
  • Parameter values are validated against operational limits, and a multi-readout provision is provided for comparing selected parameters of different flights.
  • FOQA tool with an integrated flight tracker module that plots flight paths in 2D, and an optional 3D visualization tool is available.
  • Data from various formats can be accepted, and the solution can be used for exceedance analysis, incident/accident investigation, systematic trend analysis, and reporting.
  • The software supports FTP for remote operations, with minimum human intervention.
  • Customized performance monitoring plug-in modules for fuel consumption monitoring, reduced thrust monitoring, analysis, and 3D flight animation are available, along with a web interface for remote monitoring.

Value Delivered

FliSAFE enabled airlines to carry out Flight Operations Quality Assurance (FOQA) / Helicopter Operations Monitoring Program (HOMP) effectively. It was the only software DGCA (Directorate General of Civil Aviation) certified to be on par with OEM software. FliSAFE delivered the following business benefits:

Enhanced flight safety: Early identification of potential safety risks through flight data monitoring helps prevent accidents or incidents.

Improved efficiency: Automated processing and reporting save time and effort, providing insights into aircraft performance and areas for improvement.

Regulatory compliance: Meets DGCA standards and OEM requirements.

Cost-effective: Reduces manual intervention and provides valuable insights.

Highly customizable: Supports any aircraft type and flight data recorder, with plug-in modules for customized monitoring.

NeST Digital Develops a Multi-Function Display for Fighter Aircraft

NeST Digital Develops a Multi-Function Display for Fighter Aircraft

CASE STUDY

  • AEROSPACE AND DEFENCE

Business Overview

The customer was an agency that was involved in the process of developing a combat aircraft affiliated to the defense research organization. As part of the “fly by light” avionics technology initiative, the customer wanted to develop a “Mission and Display” subsystem targeted for indigenous fighter aircraft. The scope included the development of Smart Multi-Function Displays and Head-Up Displays that supported ARINC 818 fibre optic interface.

Challenge

One of the major challenges faced, as part of the “fly by light” avionics technology initiative, was the integration of the ARINC 818 fiber optic interface. It provides a scalable and flexible solution for transmitting video, data, and control signals over fiber optic cables. However, integrating this complex interface into the Mission and Display subsystem poses several significant challenges like hardware & software complexities as well as not compromising on the overall system reliability & quality.

Solution

NeST Digital developed a Smart Multi-Function Display and Head-up Display POCs that supported ARINC 818 fiber optic interface, Ethernet interface as well as serial port communication for communicating with the subsystems and the Mission Computer. The display system used COTS processors and FPGA to implement the solution. The implementation aimed at a fast, reliable and latest technology communication interface between mission computer and display systems for aircraft avionics.

Value Delivered

  • The “fly by light” technology, which is proving to be the next-generation technology in the Aerospace domain, helps achieve a major reduction in aircraft weight by replacing the copper wire harness; it also increases the real-estate space available in the avionics bay to accommodate additional capabilities.
  • The optical communication system, being immune to Electromagnetic interference improves the aircraft safety standards and the effort required in qualification requirements for airworthy systems.
  • The redundancy achieved by replacing one set of copper cables with OFC and keeping the other set of Copper wires on standby improves system reliability.
