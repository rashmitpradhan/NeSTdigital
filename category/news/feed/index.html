Nest Group Announces Launch Of Nest Digital To Focus On Integrated Digital Transformation Solutions

KOCHI

16TH MAR 2023

Aug, 2021: The NeST group of companies announced the launch of NeST Digital, heralding its formal re-entry into the digital engineering services sector. NeST Digital combines its erstwhile IT unit NeST IT, along with its software product engineering capabilities in the Healthcare, Industrial, Transportation, Aerospace and Defense sectors into a new entity which aims to offer its customers a complete suite of total digital transformation solutions. NeST digital will funnel the group’s capabilities in SW, engineering design and vertically integrated manufacturing capabilities to offer holistic solutions.

The NeST Group, a leader in providing end to end solutions under one roof covering contract manufacturing and product engineering services, has more than three decades of experience in delivering industry-specific engineering and technology solutions for customers ranging from small and medium to Fortune 500 enterprises focusing on transportation, aerospace, defence, healthcare, power, industrial and BFSI domains.

 

Mr. N. Jehangir, MD NeST group of companies said, “As the world is moving into a more connected, data driven eco-system which requires data connectivity and intelligence on the machine, Edge device and cloud, our customers need to adapt by updating their portfolio across machine and software. NeST Digital with the support of the group’s capabilities is uniquely placed to be an efficient partner offering a single window access to design and manufacturing capabilities”.

NeST Digital will leverage NeST Cyber campus which is being rebranded as NeST Digital Academy to provide training and certification on deep technologies to both early and mid-career technology professionals. Nest Digital will expand its centers in Kochi, Trivandrum, Bengaluru and Dubai adding a new wing in its center at Pune. The company has plans to hire over 1000 professionals for its expansion needs across these centers.

Nest Digital Welcomes Employees Back To Office; New Women Friendly Policies To Come Into Place

KOCHI

28TH OCT 2022

NeST Digital announces flexible back-to-office plans as well as new women-friendly policies. Being an equal opportunity employer, helmed by a woman leader and entrepreneur Ms. Nazneen Jehangir, (CEO, NeST Digital), Nest Digital is committed to support its women workforce in making this transition.

With all the necessary precautions in place, NeST Digital is now welcoming all its employees who would want to work from the office. The model adopted is the hybrid working model which is a balance between working from the office and remotely from home, best suited amidst the ongoing global pandemic. Individual projects will choose the option to work from office or remotely.

The organization has taken various measures to build confidence among its employees. All workstations will be cleaned and sanitized frequently. WASH assessment (Workplace Assessment for Safety and Hygiene) has also been carried out in the organization.

The pandemic has brought in several new normals one of which is remote working. Companies have adapted to this and are able to work remotely with a high degree of efficiency. Having said that, work-life balance has been a challenge, especially for women. They continue to shoulder all the domestic responsibilities while managing demanding work schedules. Add to this, the real-time coaching, mentoring and career support that was always available from the office premises that went missing with the remote working situation. This unfortunately forces a lot of women to take a break from their professions. “At NeST we want to ensure that we bring in the right policies to assist them every way we can”, said Ms. Nazneen Jehangir.

Women constitute 37% of its total workforce at NeST Digital. The leadership team aspires to increase this mix through its enabling policies & practices. The Flexi work hours policy that has been brought in, aims at providing support to those who may have demanding personal schedules/commitments to manage, with flexible working hours with differential pay for limited periods. This will now be launched gender agnostic, but the key benefactors are expected to be the women workforce.

Apart from this, the management is also launching a host of other women-centric policies including; a back-towork program for women. This will be launched as part of NeST Digital’s early career hiring which will include key benefits such as; live project experience post-training, detailed assessment, feedback and counseling based on the assessment, sponsored certification programs, flexibility for long-term work from home, etc.

The NeST group of companies had previously announced the launch of NeST Digital, heralding its formal re-entry into the digital engineering services sector. NeST Digital is continuously focused on delivering integrated digital transformation solutions and plans to expand its software business. As part of their expansion plan, they have brought about new leadership to spearhead various verticals and they will now expand its centers in Kochi, Thiruvananthapuram, Bengaluru and Dubai. The company has plans to hire over 1000 professionals across all these centers.

NeST Digital, the proven provider of successful digital transformation solutions, aims to be a pioneer in the field of industry-academia partnerships.

The Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Kottayam was established as one of the “Institutions of National Importance” by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India and focuses on excellence in Information Technology, Technical Education and Research. Their mission is to generate a specialized cadre of Information Technology experts, by imparting quality education, training and to attain international standards in the fields of teaching, research and consultancy.

The joint partnership will focus extensively on the development of industry-specific solutions, leveraging the domain & tech expertise of NeST Digital in areas such as artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity, among others.

Other areas of collaboration include joint certification programmes, knowledge enrichment programmes, capacity building for NeST’s employees, setting up labs at IIIT Kottayam for research and training, job opportunities with NeST Digital and more.

The MoU signing function was held at IIIT Kottayam on November 18th, 2022 and Ms. Nazneen Jehangir, CEO and Executive Director of NeST Digital exchanged MoU documents with Prof. Rajeev Dharaskar, Director, IIIT Kottayam. The MoU stands enacted with the signatures of Dr. M Radhakrishnan, Registrar- IIIT Kottayam and Ms. Nazneen Jehangir, CEO and Executive Director of NeST Digital.
Speaking at the function, Ms. Nazneen Jehangir CEO and Executive Director of NeST Digital said “A firm believer that the best engineering minds should have access to quality education and cutting-edge R&D training, it gives me immense pleasure to collaborate with one of the finest institutions, The Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Kottayam, in fostering innovation through Industry. I’m genuinely honoured to sign this MoU as the Executive Director and CEO of NeST Digital. We, at NeST, are excitedly looking forward to a gratifying relationship with IIIT in the years to come!”

Dr. Rajiv Dharaskar, Director of IIIT Kottayam, emphasized the significance of quality education and industry-relevant research in developing cutting-edge technologies. Mr. Sivan Menon (Strategic Advisor-NeST Group), Dr. Pradeep. S.
